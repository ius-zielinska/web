Companies’ and partnerships’ legal services

Companies’ and partnerships’ service includes:
  • legal help and advice in all cases connected with corporate law, from establishing to liquidation of the company or partnership:
    • help in choosing the best form of the company or partnership to run your commercial activity in Poland,
    • preparing drafts of the articles of association and drafts of amendments to the articles of association,
    • representation in registration proceedings of the company before the registry court,
    • representation before the registry court after the company is registered in all registration proceedings required by the Polish law,
    • increase or reduction of the share capital,
    • expulsion of a shareholder,
    • mergers and acquisition of the companies,
    • dissolution and liquidation of the companies,
    • drafting resolutions of shareholders meetings, the management board or the supervisory board, as well as other corporate documents.
